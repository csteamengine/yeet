Download for macOS View source

⌘ Shift V to open · V to cycle · release to paste

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built with Tauri and Rust for speed and a tiny footprint.

⚡

Hotkey cycling

Hold your modifier keys and tap V to cycle through recent clips. Release to paste instantly into the target app.

🖼

Image support

Screenshots, area captures, copied images — all captured and pasted back as native image data, not file paths.

🔍

Smart search

Fuzzy search across your entire clipboard history. Find that snippet you copied three days ago in seconds.

🔒

Privacy first

Skips passwords, transient data, and concealed content automatically. Exclude specific apps from capture.

🎨

Native feel

Vibrancy-backed NSPanel that feels like part of macOS. No Electron, no web browser — just a fast native panel.

🛠

Configurable

Custom hotkeys, history limits, excluded apps and content types, sticky mode, paste interception — your workflow, your rules.

How it works

1

Copy anything

Yeet watches your clipboard in the background — text, URLs, code, files, images.

2

Press your hotkey

Hit ⌘⇧V to open the panel. Your history appears instantly.

3

Pick and paste

Tap V to cycle, use arrow keys, or search. Release the modifiers to paste into the active app.
